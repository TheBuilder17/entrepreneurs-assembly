About Us

Built for Entrepreneurs. By Entrepreneurs.

The Entrepreneurs Assembly is a weekly virtual gathering powered by Africa's Young Entrepreneurs (A.Y.E.) — a movement present in 20 African countries and across the diaspora in the US and UK.

Founded on the belief that limited capital and limited networks shouldn't limit ambition, the Assembly exists to close those gaps — one Saturday at a time.

Work alongside entrepreneurs solving real problems across Africa.

Network

Connect with investors, mentors and peers spanning continents.

Learn

Keynotes and fireside chats with world-class business leaders.

Grow

Access grants, training, and platforms to scale your business.

Our Vision & Mission

Empowering the Next Generation of African Leaders

We are building a thriving African entrepreneurial ecosystem where innovation, creativity, and collaboration fuel economic growth and empower communities.

  • Empower young Africans through entrepreneurship education and mentorship
  • Provide access to resources that drive economic growth and self-reliance
  • Build networks that link the diaspora with continental investment opportunities
  • Showcase and support thousands of innovative businesses and ideas
  • Champion Africa as the future of global entrepreneurship

The Founder
Dr. Summy Smart Francis
President & Founder · Africa's Young Entrepreneurs

A South African-based Nigerian entrepreneur, Dr. Summy Smart Francis started his first venture at age 14 at his father's hotel in Lagos. At 17, he managed an entire hotel branch — developing the business maturity and hunger that would shape his life's mission.

Having personally experienced the barriers of limited capital and networks, he channelled those struggles into building Africa's largest entrepreneurship movement. Today he serves as Special Adviser on Entrepreneurship & Innovation to the Ondo State Governor and is Chairman of ONDEA — Nigeria's first entrepreneurship agency.

A TEDx speaker, Harvard-trained, and holder of Nigeria's first Honorary Doctorate in Entrepreneurship — Dr. Francis is widely known as "The Godfather of Entrepreneurship."
